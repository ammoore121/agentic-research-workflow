# Research Prompts & Guidance

Use these prompts to guide comprehensive research for new projects.

## Core Research Areas

### 1. Competitive Landscape Research

**Prompt Template:**
"Search for the top 5-10 competitors in [INDUSTRY/SPACE]. For each, find:
- Product name and description
- Pricing (all tiers if available)
- Target customer segments
- Key features
- Founded date and funding (if startup)
- Website and any recent news"

**Search Strategies:**
- "[space] software" or "[space] platform"
- "[space] SaaS companies"
- "[space] startups"
- "[space] alternatives" (to find comparison sites)
- Direct searches for known competitors

**What to Capture:**
- Company name and URL
- Pricing (exact figures when available)
- Free tier or trial availability
- Feature list
- Customer testimonials/reviews
- Market positioning

### 2. Market Sizing Research

**Prompt Template:**
"Find market size and growth rates for [INDUSTRY]. Look for:
- Total addressable market (TAM) in dollars
- Current market size
- Annual growth rate
- Market forecast (5-year projections)
- Key market drivers
- Regional breakdowns (if relevant)"

**Search Strategies:**
- "[industry] market size 2024"
- "[industry] market report"
- "[industry] industry analysis"
- "[industry] market forecast"
- "[industry] growth trends"

**What to Capture:**
- Market size (dollars, units, or users)
- Growth rate (YoY)
- Source and publication date
- Key trends driving growth
- Market segments

### 3. Technology & Data Sources Research

**Prompt Template:**
"Research available data sources and APIs for [DOMAIN]. Find:
- Public datasets
- Commercial data providers
- APIs with pricing
- Data quality and coverage
- Update frequency
- Cost models"

**Search Strategies:**
- "[domain] API"
- "[domain] dataset"
- "[domain] data provider"
- "[domain] free data"
- Open data repositories (Kaggle, GitHub, etc.)

**What to Capture:**
- Data source name
- Type (API, dataset, service)
- Coverage and completeness
- Cost (free or paid)
- Update frequency
- Documentation quality

### 4. Regulatory & Compliance Research

**Prompt Template:**
"Research regulatory requirements for [INDUSTRY]. Find:
- Industry-specific regulations
- Data privacy requirements (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Licensing requirements
- Reporting obligations
- Compliance costs
- Legal risks"

**Search Strategies:**
- "[industry] regulations"
- "[industry] compliance requirements"
- "[industry] licensing"
- "[domain] privacy law"
- "[industry] legal requirements"

**What to Capture:**
- Key regulations
- Compliance complexity (low/medium/high)
- Cost of compliance
- Key risks
- Required certifications

### 5. Go-to-Market Research

**Prompt Template:**
"Research customer acquisition and market entry for [SPACE]. Find:
- Primary customer acquisition channels
- Sales cycle length
- Typical customer acquisition cost
- Pricing models in the space
- Key customer objections
- Recommended GTM strategies"

**Search Strategies:**
- "[company] customer acquisition"
- "[industry] sales cycle"
- "[industry] pricing strategy"
- "[industry] customer reviews"
- "[space] customer pain points"

**What to Capture:**
- Acquisition channels
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Sales cycle duration
- Typical pricing models
- Key customer segments

### 6. Cost/Benefit Analysis Research

**Prompt Template:**
"For [PROJECT TYPE], estimate:
- Development costs (engineering hours, tools)
- Infrastructure costs (hosting, data, processing)
- Operational costs (support, maintenance)
- Data costs (if applicable)
- Licensing costs
- Timeline to break-even"

**Search Strategies:**
- "[service] cost" or "[service] pricing"
- "[tool] implementation costs"
- "[infrastructure] pricing calculator"
- "[service] total cost of ownership"

**What to Capture:**
- One-time costs
- Recurring monthly/annual costs
- Cost per unit/user (if applicable)
- Scale assumptions
- Break-even metrics

## Research Depth Guidelines

### For Simple Projects (Quick Research)
- Identify 3-5 competitors
- Get basic market size
- Check key regulations
- Estimate high-level costs
- Timeline: 1-2 hours

### For Medium Projects (Standard Research)
- Identify 5-10 competitors with details
- Find specific market size with sources
- Map data sources and costs
- Identify key risks and mitigations
- Research GTM approaches
- Timeline: 4-8 hours

### For Complex Projects (Deep Research)
- 10+ competitors with detailed analysis
- Market sizing from multiple sources
- Complete tech/data architecture
- Full regulatory landscape
- Customer interviews or feedback synthesis
- Detailed financial modeling
- Timeline: 12+ hours

## Quality Criteria

### Bad Research Examples
- "Many competitors exist" (vague)
- "Market is growing" (no numbers)
- "Costs are reasonable" (no specifics)
- "Should be doable" (no evidence)

### Good Research Examples
- "12 competitors identified: Salesforce ($31B ARR), HubSpot ($2.1B ARR), Pipedrive ($150M+ ARR)..."
- "Market growing 18% CAGR to $157B by 2028 (Gartner 2024)"
- "Data costs $50-200/month via API, hosting $500-2000/month for MVP"
- "Similar product took 6 months with 2 engineers, technical risk: data quality"

## Source Quality Ranking

**High Quality**
- Official vendor pricing pages
- Industry analyst reports (Gartner, IDC, Forrester)
- Company earnings calls and disclosures
- Academic/government databases
- Recent news from reputable sources

**Medium Quality**
- Comparison websites (G2, Capterra, TrustRadius)
- Technical blogs with detailed analysis
- Product review sites
- Industry publications
- Government/regulatory websites

**Low Quality**
- Rumors or speculation
- Outdated sources (>2 years old)
- Unverified claims
- Opinions without data
- Competitor propaganda

Always cite your sources with URLs and publication dates.
